WHAT IS KABBLER?

Kabbler is the first subscription based ridesharing service allowing free market rates for drivers and passengers.

HOW IT WORKS

  • 1

    Subscription

    Drivers pay a subscription fee to use Kabblers proprietary software

  • 2

    Market Rates

    Drivers & Riders set minimum and maximum rates they will accept

  • 3

    Preferences

    Riders set their preferred vehicle type & age, driver gender, and more

  • 4

    Ride Matching

    A proprietary algorithm matches Drivers & Riders based on preferences

KABBLER ADVANTAGE

  • Subscription Model

    Drivers are independent business owners using Kabbler as a referral tool and NOT employees

  • Fair Market Pricing

    Riders and drivers determine fair market pricing for themselves rather than set fares being imposed on them

  • Operational Efficiency

    Market pricing eliminates the need for costly employees determining regional fare rates for different markets

  • Open Marketplace

    Kabbler is open to independent drivers as well as traditional taxi and car service drivers
